Hello! My name is Caleb Ford and my goal is to creat vivid impactfull images that force the viewer to think about the message of the photograph. Throughout my highschool career, I took photography classes in three of the four years I attended the school. This really allowed me to grow and learn as a photographer. I am currently planning to continue my photography career in college with a photography minor.

Throughout my time as a photographer I have always strived to break the norm. When you begin learning about photography it almost seems like thier is a formula for creating the perfect image if all the logistics are perfect. In reality it is harder to make more engaging images that break the mold and that is what I strive to do through my photography.
